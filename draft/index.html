Cinema names

What can we learn by aggregating first names of characters from millions of movies and tv series (since 1890th years till our days)?

How far the tv & cinema names diverge from the real life?

(Based on IMDB and "Baby names from social security card applications" data sets)

Names & Genres

The frequency of the name's occurrence depends on movie genre as well.

There are a lot of comedies with main characters called Candy, Tiffany, Randy or Chip. But it can be a bit tricky to find a commedy about Elias or Marian.

However, Anna, Teresa and Cecilia are without doubt Drama Queens and Bruce,Wong and Duke are mainly adventure guys.

Yes, there are obviously more male characters in adventure/action movies & tv series and more female characters in dramas.
